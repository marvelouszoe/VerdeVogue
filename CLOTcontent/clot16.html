Meet the makers



Making ethical choices should be easy – and we’re putting in the work so you don’t have to. Our styles are designed in Copenhagen and produced ethically in partnership with innovative factories that align with our values, and who want to make a difference in the world — for good. As protecting the environment is a collaborative effort, we’ve made sure to only team up with people who are as passionate about sustainability as we are, providing their expertise on innovative materials, technologies, and products that are even better for the Earth.
