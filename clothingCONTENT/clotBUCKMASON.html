BUCK MASON

Young Menswear Brand


This piece forgoes the traditional straight collar of a typical denim button-up jacket, opting instead for a pointed notch lapel and a longer silhouette.


Modeled after a traditional French chore coat, this jacket has three patch pockets and a soft indigo blue colour. Its sanded and pre-washed for maximum softness, and the carefully placed details of white paint drops give it a lived-in feel right off the bat.



Buck Mason CPO Jacket


Inspired by the duty uniforms of WWII-era US Navy chief petty officers, the CPO Jacket is a warm, sandy-colored number made with 60% wool, 35% polyester, and 5% recycled fiber. The top layer is brushed camel-wool twill, and it has a synthetic liner for added warmth.


Buck Mason Brushed Loopback Hooded Sweatshirt


The Brushed Loopback Hooded Sweatshirt is exactly the image that comes to mind when you think “hoodie.” With its large front pouch, sleeves that cinch at the wrist, and of course the essential hood with twin white drawstrings, this provides ultimate comfort and guaranteed warmth, whether you’re chilling at home or braving early spring or fall winds.


The intricate knitting process is designed to give your skin some separation from the fabric to promote air circulation while still keeping warmth locked in. You can get this sweater in seven different colors, including soft, off-white “Natural,” basic black, stone blue-grey “Seal Venice Wash,” and muted green “Deep Olive Venice Wash.”
