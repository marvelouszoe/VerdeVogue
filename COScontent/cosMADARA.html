Madara Cosmetics Review

Written by Sophie Diego

About Madara Cosmetics


Honest Brand Reviews

Madara Cosmetics utilizes the power of plants in its curated selection of products. Sourced from Nordic forests, this beauty brand uproots Northern-based botanicals for its patented formulas. I don’t know about you, but I’m getting some serious fairytale vibes just by looking at their makeup and skincare collection.


Madara Cosmetics is no stranger to the beauty community, as the brand boasts an impressive following of over 77.5k on Instagram. It’s also featured in various media outlets, including Forbes, Marie Claire, Vogue, and Harper’s Bazaar.


Plant-based, eco-friendly beauty is not a novel idea—which is why you’re probably wondering what makes this brand all that special. Hopefully, this Madara Cosmetics review will help answer that question for you. We’ll take an in-depth look at the company, its products, customer ratings, promotions, and more to help you decide if they’re worth checking out.


Overview of Madara Cosmetics



The magic of northern-based plants is not a matter of hocus pocus. It actually has some scientific backing, as some botanicals are enriched with key properties thanks to their strengthened adaptability to the cold Nordic climate.


Founder Lotte Tisenkopfa Iltnere wanted to highlight the benefits of these forgotten herbs as honestly as possible, which later became Madara Cosmetics in 2006.


Based in Marupe, Latvia, Madara Cosmetics has quickly amassed success for its unique approach to Nordic-based beauty.


Tisenkopfa has also won widespread recognition for her entrepreneurship efforts, such as being included in the Forbes list of 25 Most Influential Business Women in 2012. Over the years, they’ve released small collections and sub-brands, including MOSSA and ECOCERT.


Before we get into this Madara Cosmetics review, let’s go over some initial brand highlights:



Highlights


• A wide selection of skincare and makeup to choose from

• Utilizes the power of plants as a unique approach to cosmetics

• Packaging is recyclable and free of BPA materials

• Semi-affordable prices

• Provides a professionals-only program that offers exclusive discounts

• Free worldwide shipping on orders $60 or more



Stepping into a Madara Cosmetics laboratory must feel like visiting a witch’s home. That’s not to say that it’s creepy in the slightest—it’s more fascinating due to the company’s cauldron of unique ingredients. From birch water to horse chestnut seed extract, this beauty brand is definitely cooking up a storm in the name of beautiful skin.

They’ve got dozens of products on their website, ranging from serums, cleansers, foundations, mascaras, and more. You can also shop by collection or by skin concern if you’re looking for something specific.
