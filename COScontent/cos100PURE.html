The Ultimate 100% Pure Foundation Review (2024)

An Organic Beauty Brand

If you've been looking for a review that covers all of the 100% Pure Fruit Pigmented Foundations, then look no further! 100% Pure is an OG organic beauty brand with an extensive line of skincare, haircare, makeup and bodycare products that's ever expanding. Their Fruit Pigmented makeup is a tried-and-true range of foundation, lipsticks, eyeshadows, palettes and everything in between - all powered 100% by natural ingredients and fruit pigments. One of 100 Percent Pure's most popular makeup products is their foundation. Because who wants toxins and plastics sitting on their face for 8+ hours a day? 100% Pure offers a wide variety of foundations, so in this 100 Percent Pure foundation focused review I decided to break each down, so you can understand how they differ and choose the right all natural foundation for you. Read on for my 100% Pure foundation review and save 20% off with discount code OBL sitewide.



In this 100% Pure Foundation review, I am sharing my thoughts on:


100% Pure Healthy Foundation


100% Pure Water Foundation


100% Pure 2nd Skin Foundation


100% Pure Cream Foundation


100% Pure Tinted Moisturizer


100% Pure BB Cream


From various finishes to coverage and consistency to shade range, understanding all the complexion makeup options 100% Pure has to offer can seem daunting. This foundation review is meant to clear the confusion to help you figure out which foundation is the perfect fit for you. Along with a few other brands, 100% Pure is one of my top recommendations for all-natural and organic foundations. The formulas are sophisticated and the cleanest available, the performance is there and the price tags are accessible (especially with 20% off using code OBL). It's truly skincare that acts as makeup, which is what you want for a product that will be covering your entire face and sitting there for hours on end.


As you can see from the 100% Pure Foundation Swatches image above, even though I chose the lightest shades for each foundation (except for 100% Pure Water Foundation, which is 2nd to lightest), none of the shades are exactly alike.


100% Pure Full Coverage Water Foundation


For: Dry Skin


Finish: The 100% Pure website says dewy but I found this to be more off a matte finish for me, similar to Healthy Skin Foundation’s finish


Coverage: Medium to Full


Number of Shades: 19


Wear: Long lasting


The 100% Pure Water Foundation offers the most inclusive shade range of 19 different shades, provides the most coverage out of all the 100% Pure foundations, and while thick doesn’t feel overly heavy on the skin. If you’re looking for an organic foundation that also provides lots of coverage, 100% Pure’s Water Foundation is it. This would be one of my top picks out of all the foundations, my other being the Healthy Foundation if you’re not looking for substantial coverage and would like your skin to look like your skin.


Whie it says on the bottle and site that the finish is dewy, I find the 100% Pure Water Foundation finish is matte on my dry skin. One layer is all I need for good coverage. Any more and I feel the foundation would look too cakey. Instead of plastics, synthetics and possible toxins, 100% Pure Water Foundation begins in an organic Aloe Juice base and is enriched with fruit extracts, oils from Acai and Pomegranate, and skincare ingredients like Red Wine Resveratrol and Alpha Lipoic Acid.

100% Pure Cream Foundation


For: Normal, Dry, Combination Skin


Finish: Satin


Wear: Medium lasting


I tried the 100% Pure Cream Foundation in the lightest shade Alpine Rose, which turned out to be lighter than all the lightest shades across all the foundations I tried. I find it also works fantastic as an under eye brightening concealer. The coverage is somewhere between light and medium for me.


100% Pure Cream Foundation is quite dense (I live in a cold climate) and not as unctuous like some other cream foundations I've tried. This is great for me personally because I don't like a dewy finish from cream foundations. On its own the Cream Foundation doesn't seem to be as long-lasting as the other 100% Pure Foundations, but you can extend its wear by using setting or foundation powder on top.


The 100 Percent Pure Cream foundation is impressively made purely of butters, oils, rice germ powder and fruits. And by purely I mean 100%. You won't even find any minerals in this formula. When it comes to natural makeup technology, 100% Pure certainly has one of the best.


It's important to break the “seal” as in the top layer of the 100% Pure cream foundation so you can properly use the product.


Ingredients: Persea Gratissima (Avocado Butter) Oil, Punica Granatum (Pomegranate) Seed Oil, Aloe Barbadensis (Organic Aloe)* Leaf Juice, Oryza Sativa (Rice) Germ Powder, Euphorbia Cerifera (Candelilla) Wax, Pigmented Extracts of Prunus Persica (Peach) Fruit, Prunus Armeniaca (Apricot) Fruit, Theobroma Cacao (Cocoa Bean), Carica Papaya (Papaya) Fruit, Punica Granatum (Pomegranate), Vaccinium Macrocarpon (Cranberry) Fruit, Fragaria Vesca (Strawberry) Fruit, Rubus Idaeus (Raspberry) Fruit, Prunus Serotina (Wild Cherry) Fruit and Vitis Vinifera (Cabernet Grape) Fruit, Extracts of Matricaria Chamomilla (Chamomile) Leaf, Calendula Officinalis (Calendula) Flower, Camellia Sinensis (Organic White Tea) Leaf*, Vitis Vinifera (Grape) Fruit, Eucalyptus Globulus (Eucalyptus) Leaf Powder.
