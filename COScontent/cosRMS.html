RMS Beauty

Clean and Natural Color Cosmetics

About RMS Beauty


RMS Beauty makes clean, natural color cosmetics. Founded by well-known makeup artist Rose-Marie Swift, their beauty products deliver hydration and illumination to skin, helping to bring out your natural glow.


This RMS Beauty review found mentions or profiles of the brand in such top media outlets as Marie Claire, V Magazine, Allure, Tatler, Bustle, Elle, Who What Wear, and many other publications. The RMS Beauty Instagram is followed by 295k clean beauty lovers.


This RMS Beauty review will take a close look at the company’s bestselling products, customer ratings, promotions, and more to help you decide if this brand can deliver the beauty you’re looking for.



Overview of RMS Beauty



Creator of RMS Marie Swift named the brand after the initials of her full name, Rose-Marie Swift. A well-known makeup artist with decades of experience in the industry, Swift founded RMS Beauty in 2008 to provide a clean alternative to the often toxic, quick-fix makeup brands on the market.


The brand’s initials also stand for “Real Makeup Secrets” and “Right Mental State,” which speaks to RMS’ mission statement: “to create healthy luxurious beauty [using only] the highest-quality clean ingredients, meticulously sourced and tested for complete purity.”


Some of the “Real Makeup Secrets” RMS Beauty uses to make their products include using high-quality organic oils and avoiding high heats in their manufacturing process, so that the vitamins and antioxidants within the oils are protected.


Based in Charleston, South Carolina, RMS Beauty is considered a pioneer in the field of clean beauty, and RMS makeup has been used by supermodels like Miranda Kerr and Gisele Bundchen. This RMS Beauty review will now fill you in on the brand’s pros and cons.

RMS Makeup Review


This RMS Beauty review will take you through some of the brand’s bestselling products, including powerful but lightweight concealers, shimmery luminizers and bronzers to accentuate your features, and helpful tools that make the application process easier.


RMS UnCover-Up


Some days our skin looks perfect—there’s not a blemish in sight and we’re really feeling ourselves, no makeup required. Fast forward a few days and it’s a whole different story: dark circles and red spots appear, and we wonder how our skin could change up on us so fast.


“Un” Cover-Up camouflages those areas you’d rather the world not see, but not in the way those thick and heavy concealers do. The RMS uncover up concealer uses auto-adjusting minerals for a lightweight feel and a natural finish that lets you go about your day with confidence.


The RMS Beauty un cover-up concealer/foundation is available in 16 shades ranging from light alabaster to rich ebony, and retails for $36.


RMS UnCover-Up Cream Foundation


Foundation is one of those things that many women can’t leave the house without. It provides a smooth, even look that makes us look a little more kempt. Whether you like just a touch of coverage or a more pristine look, “Un” Cover-Up Cream Foundation is buildable to your preference.


Available in 16 shades, this lightweight RMS foundation contains skin-loving ingredients like organic coconut oil and wild-crafted buriti oil to nourish and calm skin while. The antioxidant-packed “Un” Cover-Up Cream Foundation comes in a 30ml container for $52.


RMS Mascara


Whether you’re going for a full, vibrant look or dramatic definition, RMS Mascara has you covered. RMS’ volumizing shade gives your lashes a natural curl and thickness, while the defining shade separates and intensifies your lashes to make even more of an impression.


Made with concentrated mineral pigments and organic oils, this Mascara delivers eye-opening effects, defining your eyes’ shape and giving the finishing touch to makeup looks. This long-wearing natural mascara is $28.
